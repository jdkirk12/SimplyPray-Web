The ACTS Framework

A time-tested path
through prayer.

Four movements that bring structure, Scripture, and depth to every prayer session — in 15 minutes or less.

A
Step 1
Adoration

Begin by focusing on who God is. Scripture-backed attributes guide you in praising God for His character — not just His gifts.

C
Step 2
Confession

Guided reflection prompts honest naming of sin, and the freedom that comes from bringing it into the light before God.

T
Step 3
Thanksgiving

Name God's faithfulness. Record answered prayers and build a living record of His goodness you can return to for years.

S
Step 4
Supplication

Organized lists for family, church, work, and more. Tag, filter, and pray through what matters most — nothing falls through.

Built Different

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

No feeds. No algorithms. No streak guilt. Just the tools a person actually needs to pray — organized around how Christians have prayed for centuries.

Prayer Lists

Organize requests by category — family, church, work, missions. Tag and filter so nothing falls through the cracks.

Answered Prayers

When God answers, move it to your thanksgiving list. Build a record of faithfulness you can look back on for years.

Tag & Browse

Pray by theme in any session. Browse your full prayer history by tag — health, healing, finances, missions, and more.

Distraction-Free

Built for iOS Focus mode. No social feeds, ads, or push alerts. Just you, God, and the prayers that matter most.

Scripture-Rooted

Every step is anchored to God's Word — divine attributes, biblical confessions, and promises that fuel real prayer.

Private by Default

Your prayers are yours. Local-first storage, optional iCloud sync. We never see, sell, or share your prayer data.
